Root Canal Treatment at Aarthys Dentistry

At Aarthys Dentistry, we specialize in Root Canal Treatment (RCT) to save damaged or infected teeth, relieving pain and restoring dental health. Our aim is to provide effective treatments that preserve your natural smile.

What is Root Canal Treatment?

Root Canal Treatment is a dental procedure used to treat infection or damage to the pulp (the innermost part) of a tooth. It involves removing the infected pulp, cleaning and disinfecting the interior of the tooth, and sealing it to prevent further infection.

When is RCT Needed?

  • Severe Toothache: Persistent pain in the tooth may indicate pulp damage.
  • Infection or Abscess: Presence of an abscess or swelling around the tooth.
  • Trauma: Injury to a tooth that may have caused pulp damage.
  • Deep Decay: Extensive decay that has reached the pulp area.

The RCT Process

  • Initial Consultation: We evaluate the tooth and take necessary X-rays to determine the extent of damage.
  • Local Anesthesia: The area around the tooth is numbed for your comfort during the procedure.
  • Pulp Removal: The infected pulp is carefully removed to clean the interior of the tooth.
  • Cleaning and Shaping: The canal is cleaned, disinfected, and shaped for filling.
  • Sealing the Tooth: The cleaned canal is filled and sealed to prevent future infections.
  • Follow-Up: A follow-up visit may be required to place a crown for further protection.

Post-Treatment Care

After RCT, it’s important to maintain good oral hygiene, avoid hard foods for a few days, and follow any specific care instructions given by our team to ensure healing and long-term success.
