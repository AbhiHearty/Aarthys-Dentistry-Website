Dental Braces at Aarthys Dentistry

At Aarthys Dentistry, we offer orthodontic solutions like braces to help align and straighten teeth, improve bite functionality, and enhance overall dental health. Our goal is to provide you with a beautifully aligned and healthy smile.

What are Dental Braces?

Dental braces are devices used in orthodontics to correct alignment issues such as crowded or crooked teeth, overbites, underbites, and gaps. They work by applying continuous pressure, gradually moving teeth into proper alignment over time.

Types of Braces We Offer

  • Metal Braces: Traditional metal braces are durable and effective for various alignment issues.
  • Ceramic Braces: These braces blend with your natural tooth color, making them less noticeable.
  • Lingual Braces: Positioned on the back of the teeth, these braces are virtually invisible from the front.
  • Clear Aligners: Removable, clear plastic trays that provide a discreet way to straighten teeth.

We offer a range of braces to fit each patient’s unique needs and preferences, ensuring optimal results and comfort.

Why Choose Aarthys Dentistry for Braces?

  • Customized Treatment Plans: We tailor our orthodontic approach to meet your specific dental needs and lifestyle.
  • Advanced Technology: Our clinic uses modern technology to ensure precise and comfortable braces treatments.
  • Experienced Team: Our skilled dentists provide expert guidance and support throughout the braces process.

The Braces Treatment Process

  • Initial Consultation: A comprehensive examination helps us evaluate your orthodontic needs and select the best braces option.
  • Fitting the Braces: The braces are carefully placed on the teeth, with adjustments to ensure comfort and alignment.
  • Regular Adjustments: We schedule follow-up visits to adjust the braces and monitor progress.
  • Completion and Retainers: Once alignment is achieved, the braces are removed, and retainers are provided to maintain the results.

Post-Treatment Care

After your braces are removed, it’s essential to wear retainers as advised to keep your teeth aligned. Good oral hygiene and regular dental visits help ensure lasting results.
